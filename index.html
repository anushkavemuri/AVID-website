AV Institute of Design

About Us :

For more than a century, AVID has been inspired by the transformative potential of design. Today, the school’s groundbreaking academic programs carry forward that mission, making AVID the number one design school in India and among the top three globally. At the heart of a comprehensive university — The New School — AVID draws on a range of academic fields to deliver a uniquely interdisciplinary education enriched by the resources of its home in Hyderabad and branch campus in . Here, the next generation of creative leaders engage in real-world learning and develop as critical thinkers and makers grappling with the pressing issues of our time.

Courses Offered-Overview

Architectural Design (BFA)

This program offers students a rigorous pre-professional design education that incorporates collaboration, research, and critical thinking. Students develop expertise in spatial, digital, ecological, and material design through a curriculum that explores the design process from modeling to material construction. CAREER OPPORTUNITIES: ~ Architecture ~ Landscape and Urban Design ~ Exhibition Design ~ Interior Design ~ Environmental Enterprises ~ Graduate Study

Fine Arts (BFA)

AVID Fine Arts program prepares young artists to make an original contribution to our visual culture and traditions. Students explore color, form, space, and composition, but curricula also emphasize aesthetic inquiry, interdisciplinary approaches to creating artwork, and social engagement. Students interact at many levels with the New York City and international art world. CAREER OPPORTUNITIES ~ Fine Art ~ Arts Administration ~ Curation ~ Museum Management ~ Arts Criticism

Theory and History of Art and Design

Embracing a range of media and perspectives, Theory and History of Art and Design stresses critical thinking and analysis. Concentrators gain clarity of written and verbal communication and learn artistic expression’s historical and cultural value.The Theory and History of Art and Design (THAD) department offers thought-provoking courses focused on a wide range of media, spanning time periods from ancient to contemporary and embracing diverse critical perspectives.

Fashion Design

This program — offered both in Paris and in Hyderabad — develops students' design-thinking and creative problem-solving strategies as they explore form, silhouette, material, and process. Students create innovative, sustainable, ethically made, and beautiful fashion for global markets. CAREER OPPORTUNITIES ~ Fashion Design ~ Styling ~ Merchandising ~ marketing-communications ~ Public Relations ~ Graduate Study

Interior Design

A leader in interior design education, We take a research-oriented approach to the creation of physical spaces. Students work in small groups designing a range of interiors. Graduates are prepared to enter established design and architecture studios, open their own design practices, and work as designers and consultants with public and private institutions. CAREER OPPORTUNITIES ~ Interior Design ~ Sustainable Design ~ Set Design ~ Exhibition Design ~ Historic Preservation ~ Consulting ~ Graduate Study

Photography

Aspiring professional photographers develop technical and conceptual skills as they explore analog and digital technologies. Students focus on fine art, fashion, commercial, or editorial photography and benefit from opportunities to take part in exhibitions, studio visits, and projects with international collaborators. Parsons connects the craft of photography with design, fine art, and film and video.
