AV Institute of Design

About Us :

For more than a century, AVID has been inspired by the transformative potential of design. Today, the school’s groundbreaking academic programs carry forward that mission, making AVID the number one design school in India and among the top three globally. At the heart of a comprehensive university — The New School — AVID draws on a range of academic fields to deliver a uniquely interdisciplinary education enriched by the resources of its home in Hyderabad and branch campus in the U.S. Here, the next generation of creative leaders engage in real-world learning and develop as critical thinkers and makers grappling with the pressing issues of our time.
